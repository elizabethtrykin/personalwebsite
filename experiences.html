Experiences

Intern at Morneau Shepell's Children Support Solutions

I worked with behaviour + occupational therapists and speech-language pathologists, helping autistic children. I learned to communicate with children that struggled to interact with the outside world. Folowing the internship, I built a web-app for non-verbal toddlers that use pictures and images to communicate. The algorithm was exactly like a text suggestion, but used images instead.

Consulting, The Dariu Foundation

My team and I built and implemented a recommendation for the The Dariu Foundation for "The Impossible Project", aiming to teach digital skills to 7 million children in Southeast Asia.

Private tutor, turning it into a saas business

Tutoring one student at a time (math, science, Essay writing, prep for special high-school programs etc.) became extremely tedious and time-consuming, so I eventually built a software with a subscription model for the students. I from 20 hours/week with 10 students to only 10 hours/week, with about 35 students at its peak.

LabDAO

Brainstormed the idea of LabDAO with the founder before it became an established DAO. The goal of the project is to create an open community of CROs (Contract Research Organizations) to outsource wet & dry lab experiments. I have been contributing to the open-community project since.
